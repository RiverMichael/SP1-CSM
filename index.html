Explore the world of science

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we are committed to making science accessible to all

Must-see exhibits

Cosmology

Our fantastic exhibition, "The Sky Above Us" explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us.

Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future.

Ecology

We unpack the dangers we currently face including global warming, extinction of species, and pollution.

Young Stars Club

Every Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 our Young Stars Club member's meets to explore and experiment together with our team of experts.

Don't worry! It's free to be a YoungStar. All you have to do is sign up at our information desk at the museum.

As a Young Star member you also get a free t-shirt, baseball cap and priority to our popular Holiday Clubs.
