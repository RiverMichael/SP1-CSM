Upcoming events

Night in the museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays.

Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring and sleep over beside dinosaurs.

Aeronautics and space

Professor Sheila Widnall delivers a lecture on aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting “space”.

Energetica exhibition

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam.

Experience the power of elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering, lighting, to "Wind Island" which shows how turbines are able to use and control the wind to create power.

Holiday club

During the school holidays, we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore.

To find out more about our holiday club and how you can join, navigate into our contact page and send us a message.

Birthdays

We would be happy to host your birthday party at the museum.

Start the birthday with a guided tour around the museum, interact with the exhibits and learn more about science.

End the party with cake and beverages in our museum café while you open your gifts.
