Admission

We believe science should be avalible to everyone.

Therefor the admission to the museum is FREE for all.

Explore the museum

Kids

Learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth?

Join our Young Stars Club or why not spend your next holiday learning more about science in our Holiday Club

The family

Our interactive exhibits offer interesting experiences for the whole family, not just for the young ones.

Bring the family and explore the world of science and enjoy a healthy lunch at our café.

Teachers

Our team is on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them.

Please get in touch with us here to find out more about our facilities and to arrange a time to bring your students through for a visit.

Shop & Dine

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.

Dine

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Here you can also meet our serving robot Waitressia.

Guided Tours

We offer hourly guided tours of the museum. For groups of 6 people or more, please contact us to arrange the tour. The tour cost 70 NOK per person and include a printed guide to the museum.

Accessibility

Our museum is for everybody. We have wheelchair accessibility ramps and we offer audio guides and braille display signs.
